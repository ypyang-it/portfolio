My Journey in Computer Programming

My name is Yupeng Yang, and I am currently studying Computer Programming at Saint Paul College in St. Paul, Minnesota. I am working toward my Associate of Applied Science degree with an expected graduation date of May 2027, and I currently maintain a 4.0 GPA as well as being a member of the Phi Theta Kappa Honor Society. I created this website as a portfolio to showcase my growing skills in programming and web development.

So far, I have been learning HTML and CSS, along with foundational concepts in networking and computer systems. I am also working toward my Cisco CCNA certification to strengthen my understanding of networking and real-world IT environments. My long-term goal is to continue my education by pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and eventually a Master’s degree in Artificial Intelligence.

Before returning to school, I gained professional experience in technical support, data analysis, as well as leadership experience as a lead framing carpenter. These roles helped me develop strong problem-solving skills, attention to detail, and the ability to work effectively in fast-paced environments. Outside of school, I enjoy working on home remodeling projects and continuing to learn new technical skills.
